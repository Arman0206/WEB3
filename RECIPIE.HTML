Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil

Recipie

  1. Take the gulab jamun pre mix out of the packet. Knead the dough adding warm milk. Cut small portion balls out of in it
  2. Make small ball and no cracks on it. Heat a kadai adding ghee. Fry on low to medium flame. Add small quantity of balls as they will become large in size as fried.
  3. Now on another side put a pan add sugar and water to prepare sugar syrup. Fry the balls it dark brown as they will become light in colour after dipped in syrup.
  4. As all balls are fried, add in a ready and hot sugar syrup. Cover the lid and let them absorb the syrup for over night. Next day you will find that our gulab jamuns are juicy and tasty.

Barfi

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Milk Powder
  • Ghee
  • Milk

Recipie

  1. Heat the ghee in a pan. Add milk. Stir.
  2. Add milk powder. Stir and cook on low flame till it thickens.
  3. Add kesar Elaichi. Stir continuously and cook till it leaves the sides. Add a little bit ghee.
    4. Allow it to set and cut into pieces. Garnish with silver leaf or chopped pistachios.
